About Fixify

A capstone project by Holy Angel University students — reimagining how homeowners connect with trusted service professionals in Angeles City.

Our Mission

Fixify was born out of a simple observation: finding a reliable home service professional in Angeles City shouldn't be difficult. Too often, homeowners rely on word of mouth, and skilled tradespeople struggle to reach new clients.

We built Fixify to bridge that gap — a platform where verified providers can showcase their skills, and customers can book services with confidence, knowing their payment is protected until the job is done right.

This project was developed as the final requirement for 6AWEB — Advanced Dynamic Web Development at Holy Angel University, Angeles City, under the guidance of Dr. Joseph A. Esquivel.
